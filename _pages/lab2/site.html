More About Speedcubing

Speedcubing is the practice of solving Rubik's cubes and similar puzzles as quickly as possible.

For official competitions and rankings, visit the World Cube Association.

Speedcubing Resources

Popular Cube Brands

GAN

Premium cubes with innovative designs and magnetic systems.

MoYu

Well-known for WeiLong series and budget-friendly options.

QiYi

Makers of the popular Thunderclap series and magnetic puzzles.

DaYan

Historic brand famous for the ZhanChi that revolutionized cubing.

Solving Methods

CFOP

The most popular method: Cross, F2L, OLL, PLL. Used by most champions.

Roux

Block-building approach with fewer moves and more intuitive solving.

ZZ

Focuses on edge orientation early for efficient rotationless solving.

Competition Categories

Speed Events

Standard timed solves with 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, and other cubes.

Special Events

One-handed, blindfolded, multi-blind, and fewest moves challenges.

Practice Schedule

Beginner

Practice 20 minutes daily focusing on basic algorithms and finger tricks.

Intermediate

Practice 1 hour daily with algorithm drills, timed solves, and slow solving for lookhead practice.
